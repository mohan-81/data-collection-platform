This connector uses your Taboola Backstage credentials for maximum privacy and control. Campaign summary, top campaign content, and publisher revenue reports are fetched from Taboola APIs.

Credentials are encrypted using AES-256 before being stored in your secure vault.

How to get Client ID & Secret

  • Generate Backstage API credentials in your Taboola account.
  • Use OAuth2 Client Credentials with your Client ID and Client Secret.
  • Set a default Account ID from your allowed accounts list.
  • No redirect URI is required for this connector flow.
